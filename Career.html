Computer and Information Research Scientist
According to www.bls.gov, a Computer and Information Research Scientist will usually explore issues in computing, improve on software systems, and work with advanced algorithms. A Computer and Information Research Scientist will need at least a doctorate or professional degree. The projected job outlook for 2014-24 is 11%. And the median pay for 2015 was $110,620 per year or $53.18 per hour.
